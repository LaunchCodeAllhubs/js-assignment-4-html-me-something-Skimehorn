Jared's Top 5 Games

My name is Jared and this is my top 5 favorite games/series! Ive been playing video games most of my life and started playing board games as well in 2018. This is a list of video and board games I think are worth giving a try.

The Legend of Zelda

Ill start by saying all games in the series are worth playing and are favorites among millions. However, my favorites would be the 2 most recent, Breath of the Wild and Tears of the Kingdom. Both are open world games that give the series a fresh approach to the rpg style and really allow you the freedom of exploration. If you want to lose yourself for a month or two these are the games for you.

Gloomhaven

Gloomhaven is a tactical combat in a persistent world of shifting motives. Players will take on the role of a wandering adventurer with their own special set of skills and their own reasons for traveling to this dark corner of the world. It is a tabletop turn based RPG which can be played solo or up to 4 players. Perfect for people who enjoy games like DnD.

Tomb Raider Series

This series includes Tomb Raider, Rise of the Tomb Raider, and Shadow of the Tomb Raider. The three games tell a well-written, sympathetic, exciting, beautiful and just incredibly well-made story about everyones favorite tomb raider, Lara Croft. Single player and story driven with action packed game play that really immerses you in world with realistic and supernatural elements.

Ghost of Tsushima

This 2020 action adventure game is not only packed full of samurai action and fluid gameplay but is also a cinematic experience that really pulls you into the world its building. Single player adventure with many different styles of play and weapons to use.

Wonderland's War

With 2-5 players take the role as a faction leader who has been invited to the Hatter's tea party. Drink tea and eat cake as you move around the table drafting cards to gather your forces, build your towers, upgrade your leader, and recruit Wonderlandians to your cause.
